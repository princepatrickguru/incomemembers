_____   FAQs

Does the daily profit transfer to my currency account directly?

No, earnings are accumulated on your INCOME MEMBER account and are always available for withdrawal at the end of each month.

Do I always have access to my capital?

No, you have the choice to withdraw money from your account only at the end of each month. However, you must submit your request five days prior to the close of the trading session.

Do I receive my first monthly profit payout 30 days after joining?

No, the payout day in INCOME MEMBER is always the last day of the month. In other words, regardless of when you became an investor with INCOME MEMBER, your payout day will always be on the 30th of the first month.

Do I receive my entire monthly profit payout for each month?

Our payment schedule displays the total profit distribution for each trading term of 30 days. For 30 trading days, the entire daily profit is paid to you. However, if your investment portfolio is activated on the 17th trading day, you will only receive profits payout for the 18th through the 30th trading day.

In what ways can I put money into my INCOME MEMBER account?

You can only deposit money into your account using a cryptocurrency or other type of digital currency. We take Perfect Money, Trc20 USDT, etc.

What are the approved methods for withdrawal on Income Members?

Withdrawals can be done to a local bank account by wire transfer, cryptocurrency, or e-currency.

When will the money appear in my account once I request a withdrawal?

Within 12 to 48 business hours, funds are often available.

Can I set up many accounts in your program?

No. There is no purpose in creating additional accounts. if you already have an active investment and want to add more money to it. Simply make a deposit to your INCOME MEMBER account and apply for or activate a new investment portfolio on your account. Up to two active investment portfolios are allowed on your account. It should be noted that regardless of when they were activated, every active portfolio will receive a profit dividend at the end of the month.

Can I take my gains out whenever I want?

Your daily earnings total will appear under the currently active portfolio on your account dashboard. Every day, this profit is rising. However, you can only have access to the earnings at the end of 30 trading days.

Do I have the option to withdraw my money whenever I want?

Your daily earnings total will be displayed on your account dashboard under the currently active portfolio. Daily gains will be made. However, you can only access the fund after 30 trading days have passed.
